The following files are used directly in GEOCARB_NET


(1) Glacial_Threshold.csv

Calculated glacial inception thresholds with 6, 4.5. and 3 degrees climate sensitivity. Assumes a modern threshold of 500 ppm and a dimmer sun in the past.

(2) Isotope_Time_Series_2023.csv

Experimental:

Column 2 and 3 include refined d13C values (Carmer and Javus 2020) but they are heavily smoothed and only column 2 is used in the software. Experimental refinedments of Sr and d34S not used in the software.
